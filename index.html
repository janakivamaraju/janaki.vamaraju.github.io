I am a Ph.D candidate at the Jackson School of Geosciences, UT Austin. I am advised by Dr. Mrinal Sen. I am also affiliated with the University of Texas Institute for Geophysics. I enjoy developing accurate and efficient solutions to real world problems using mathematical physics. Currently, my research involves solving forward and inverse problems in exploration geophysics. I have had the good fortune of working with wonderful researchers.
